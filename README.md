# Gladiator-Project
Welcome to the Gladiator Project!

Week 1:
The idea for the game for the first week is that we want a basic functional game to base of future developments.

City/Hub:
The main city is the place where the Player will always find place. In the city the player can find 3 shops, armor shop, weapon shop and a
magic shop. There he can buy items to futher assist him in his fights.

Shops:
There are 3 shops. The player can wonder in every shop and buy a desired item. For now there will be 5 items per shop to buy.
5 weapons, 5 armor pieces and 5 spells. The player will begin with a certain amount of gold.

Arena:
This is the place where the player can fight opponents. For now let's make only 3 opponents to fight with. After every succesfull match
the player will gain gold and experience to level up. 

Inventory:
The player will have a basic inventory system. He can hold one piece of armor, one weapon and one magic spell. For now he won't be able to buy multiple 
weaposn/armor pieces/magic spells at once and he cannot stash them. This will be updated in future weeks.

Economic system:
The player will have a "wallet" with gold. He can buy items or try to buy something, but then he'll get refused because he has not enough gold. 
He will also get gold after finishing a fight.

Player class:
The program should have a player class where all the player logic will be implemented. For now we want for the player to be able to buy items, add the to his inventory and
we want him to be able to participate in the arena fights. Attack logic should be implemented in this week.

Enemy class:
For this week there will be only 3 enemys in the arena battles. Each enemy has his statistics and items.
